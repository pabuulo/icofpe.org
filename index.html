OUR STORY

The International Committee of Politics and Education (ICofPE) is a global organization dedicated to enhancing the quality of education worldwide. Founded in the United Kingdom, we now engage with member associations, universities, professionals, and educators across more than 60 countries. Our efforts focus on promoting education at all levels and collaborating with governments to address national educational needs.

Our vision is to foster national educational movements and increase international collaboration with Goverments and partner universities. The ICofPE works to create scholarship opportunities and nation-wide changes. We aim to expand the range of available courses and provide accessible, high-quality education for all.

The ICofPE began with a small group of dedicated professionals and has since expanded its reach by partnering with numerous associations worldwide. These partnerships help us connect with students and better understand their needs. Over time, we have strengthened our collaboration with governments across the globe, working to improve access to education and ensure that students everywhere benefit from high-quality learning opportunities.
